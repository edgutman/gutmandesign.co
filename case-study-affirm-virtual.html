Product Design Growth Mobile Virtual Payments

Redesigned virtual card onboarding to make Buy Now, Pay Later more accessible and appealing

Challenge

Affirm's virtual card feature had low adoption despite being a key differentiator for Buy Now, Pay Later. Users found the setup process confusing and didn't understand the benefits.

Solution

Complete redesign of the virtual card creation and management experience, with clearer value proposition, streamlined setup, and better integration into shopping flows.

Impact

Nearly 4% increase in split pay loan creation, representing significant revenue growth and improved product-market fit for virtual payment solutions.

Process Highlights

User Research

Understanding why users weren't adopting the virtual card feature despite its clear benefits.

  • Analyzed user behavior data to identify drop-off points
  • Conducted usability studies on existing virtual card flow
  • Interviewed customers about payment preferences and concerns
  • Competitive analysis of virtual card experiences

Design Strategy

Creating a more compelling and trustworthy virtual card experience.

  • Front-loaded value proposition with clear benefit explanation
  • Simplified card creation process with fewer required steps
  • Improved visual design to build trust and credibility
  • Better integration with merchant checkout flows

Key Improvements

Specific design changes that drove adoption and usage.

  • Clearer explanation of how virtual cards work and their benefits
  • Streamlined card generation with automatic population of details
  • Enhanced security messaging to build user confidence
  • Improved card management interface for ongoing use

Key Learnings

  • Trust signals are crucial in financial product design
  • Reducing steps doesn't always mean removing information
  • Integration with existing flows matters more than standalone features
